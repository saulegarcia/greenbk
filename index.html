The Plan to make Brooklyn go Green!

We propose to create a grant or loan that will allow Brooklyn residents to pay off the initial installment fee charge by companies for the transition to a new form of renewable energy source. This grant will give the Residents a time of 10 years to pay off, while the gain profits from going green. Solar energy is set to play an essential role in removing fossil fuels from renewable energy sources. Solar energy works through the use of solar voltaic glass panels to generate power, thus saving costs and high related costs associated with electricity bills. Therefore, solar power is a crucial component to the removal of the borough of Brooklyn's fossil fuel. In this business proposal plan, I have discussed why the borough of Brooklyn should go green by using solar energy.
